Model Architecture
01
Team Strength Parameters
FIP/xFIP for pitchers, wRC+ for offenses, Dixon-Coles α/β for soccer. All park and league adjusted.
02
Game State Simulation
Markov chain for MLB (24 base-out states), Poisson xG shot-by-shot for soccer. Models sequential structure correctly.
03
Low-Score Correction
Dixon-Coles ρ correction for soccer scorelines. Bullpen handoff model and fatigue curves for MLB starters.
04
Contextual Adjustments
Park factors, platoon splits (L/R), injury modifiers, home field advantage, weather and stadium type.
05
Monte Carlo Output
100,000 iterations per game. Full probability distributions across moneyline, spread, totals, props, and 1H markets.
06
Parlay Builder
Cross-game parlay construction from LOCK/HIGH/LEAN signals. Ranked by joint probability, fair odds shown.
